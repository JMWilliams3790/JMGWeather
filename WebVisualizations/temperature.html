Latitude Vs Temperature

The most obvious observation from the data shows that as cities are closer to the equator the temperature tends to rise. The data was collected earlier in the year. What is interesting about this is that the cities in the southern hemisphere tend to be warmer than their northern counterparts. This may be due to the tilt of the planet on it's axis.
